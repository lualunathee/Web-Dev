Recipe #1. Cheese Sandwich

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Bread 1 slice
Cheese 2 slice
Butter 1 tbsp

Steps:

  1. Butter one side of each bread slice.
  2. Place the cheese slices between the unbuttered sides of the bread.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium heat; place the sandwich buttered-side-down
  4. Cook for a few minutes
  5. Slice and enjoy

Recipe #2. Cucumber Salad

  • Time: 10-15 minutes
  • Serves: 1-2

Steps:

  1. Wash and slice the cucumber and tomato
  2. Place them in a bowl and sprinkle a little salt to taste.
  3. Slice and enjoy
Ingredients Quantity
Cucumber 1 whole
Tomato 1 medium
Salt to taste
